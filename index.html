2017 will be a year I'll never forget.

Welcome

The year 2017 has been a year that has brough about may changes and one in which I had more downs and at the same time more ups than any other year. It all began when in December of 2016 when I moved back to the United States after spending over five years in my native country of Trinidad '&' Tobago. I would then move to Clearwater for my son to attend school at the "National Aviation Academy" With no onsite housing we had to make some tough choices to accomidate both our dream for a better tomorrow. and the adventures along the way make for an unforgetable year.

A Crazy Adventure

The year would start by my living in my mother's car for about four months as my son Christopher and I looked for a place to call home. School for him had already begun and having been a home schooling mother for the past seven years, finances were none existant, but we had to do something different in order to make things work out and this was it. Along the way the windshield would shatter making the car unsafe to drive...it would take two week before we were able to get it fixed. Each day on the road was a pray and a hope that it did not come in on us for as we drove from point 'A' to 'B' fragments of glass would fall on us.

Celebrations

By April Christopher had passed his first national exam and had aced all major exnamination given in class. To add to it all we were able to secure an aparement and not a moment too soon.

My Family

As in many spanish cultures, family garthering are extreamly special times and my family meet almost on a weekly basis. Althoug we were stying in Clearwater, we would venture weekly back to Palm Bay, Florida to spend time with our family members.

Special Moments

The sunrise and sunsets on the Clearwater side of Florida would prove to be the thing that gave us the most peace during our time outside of a home. I would also go back into teaching, and to top it off I was looking for a way to get back into the technology field...that's when I found LaunchCode.
